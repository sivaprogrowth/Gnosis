India's **first commercial peer-to-peer lending platform** (dhanax.com). Originally focused on unbanked populations and self-help groups. Shut down after scaling to a few thousand customers due to regulatory uncertainty.

## Why it's in this wiki

[[siva-cotipalli]]'s **first startup** — Co-Founder from Sep 2007 to Dec 2010 (~3 years). Formative founder experience: developed business plan, managed operations and cash flow, raised an angel round, built a 14-person team, ran PR and partnerships.

## Key outcomes

- Helped 5,000+ small business people raise initial capital.
- Channelized ₹3.5 Cr+ to low-income women entrepreneurs.
- Raised an angel round.
- 16 national/international media features.

## Why it ended

"Lack of clarity on the regulatory environment" (Siva's own framing on LinkedIn). Predates India's P2P lending regulatory framework by several years.
