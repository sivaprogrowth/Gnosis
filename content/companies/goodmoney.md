A debt-financing platform for small business owners and individuals in India. Built on data-science-driven underwriting to simplify loan acquisition for both borrowers and lenders. Positioned as a "financial friend" for consumers and small-business owners.

## Why it's in this wiki

[[siva-cotipalli]] was **Head of Product** from 2014 to 2016 (~2 years) — his **second fintech role** after [[dhanax]] (India's first P2P lending platform, 2007–2010). Between the two, Siva has ~5 years of hands-on fintech product leadership covering both peer-to-peer and institutional debt-financing models.

Sibling context to [[dhanax]]: both fintech, both founder/product-leader roles, both focused on the under-banked segment in India. Together they anchor Siva's credibility in the financial-services vertical — one of [[progrowth]]'s primary ICP categories today.
