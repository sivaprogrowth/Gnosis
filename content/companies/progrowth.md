An AI-first fractional marketing operations company. Operates as an embedded marketing function for B2B companies rather than advising from the outside.

## Services

- **Fractional CMO** — embedded marketing leadership, not offshore advisory.
- **AI marketing** — LLM-assisted content production, AI video generation, AI-native SEO (GEO).
- **Marketing automation** — CRM (Zoho Bigin), booking systems, SMS/email reminder pipelines, ops tooling.
- **AI video generation & editing** — short-form creative for LinkedIn, paid media, sales enablement.
- **Analytics & attribution** — Matomo (self-hosted), Google Analytics, custom dashboards.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Mid-market B2B in verticals that are historically underserved by modern marketing tooling:

- B2B tech / SaaS (early- to mid-growth stage)
- Financial services (commercial lending, wealth, insurance brokerages)
- Credit unions and community banks
- Insurance carriers and agencies
- Professional services (legal, accounting, consulting)

## Strategic bet

Three structural shifts converge in ProGrowth's favor:

1. **AI-search disruption.** Traditional SEO assumptions break (see [[earned-media-bias]], [[brand-strength-ai-visibility-gap]]). Clients need operators who can execute a [[generative-engine-optimization]] playbook, not just talk about it.
2. **Fractional is structurally better than full-time for mid-market.** The $300K/year CMO doesn't fit a $3–15M revenue business. A fractional operator running an AI-augmented stack does.
3. **Toolchain is productizable.** The same stack ProGrowth runs for clients ([[ai-overview-tool]], Matomo, Bigin automations, Gnosis) becomes an asset.

## Competitive positioning

- vs [[marketri]] — pure advisory, no embedded execution, weak on AI ([[marketri]] page has deeper breakdown).
- vs [[semrush]] — Semrush ships tools; ProGrowth ships *outcomes*. Complementary rather than competitive for now.
- vs generalist fractional firms — ProGrowth is AI-native by default.

# Current focus (April 2026)

- AI-search visibility tooling ([[ai-overview-tool]]) — productizing what the team does manually for clients
- Content marketing operation — programmatic SEO for ProGrowth-owned properties, establishing thought leadership in GEO category
- Hackathon POC of [[gnosis-hackathon]] — demonstrating the firm's AI-native positioning
