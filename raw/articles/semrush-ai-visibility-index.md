# Semrush AI Visibility Index — page overview (fetched 2026-04-19)

## What it is

Semrush's AI Visibility Index is a benchmarking tool measuring brand visibility across AI search platforms. Tagline: *"who's winning in AI search and how they're doing it"* — by analyzing how LLMs recommend and mention brands.

## Methodology and scope

- **Sample size:** 2,500 prompts analyzed across 5 major industries.
- **Platforms covered:** ChatGPT and Google AI Mode.
- **Data points tracked:**
  - Visibility trends by sector.
  - Performance across AI platforms.
  - Brand mentions in LLM-generated answers.
  - Co-occurrence patterns with competitors.
  - Cited sources AI pulls from.

## Key metrics

- **Share of Voice (SoV)**: accounts for mention frequency and position in answers. *"If a brand was mentioned first in every AI answer, they would achieve an SoV of 100%."*
- **Brand Diversity**: number of brands comprising 80% of vertical visibility — higher = more competitive market.
- **Source Diversity Score**: unique sources ÷ total prompts per vertical.

## Key findings surfaced on the page

- Users searching with LLMs convert **4.4× more** than those using traditional search engines. (No primary source cited.)
- AI-generated results projected to overtake organic search by 2028.
- ChatGPT: 2.5 billion daily prompts from 190+ million users.
- Data updated monthly; latest data as of April 2026.

## Interactivity

Filterable by AI platform, vertical, and date. Brand leaderboards, competitor co-occurrence, top cited sources — all visualized per filter.
