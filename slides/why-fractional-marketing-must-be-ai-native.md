## The AI-Search reality for challenger brands

> 62.2% of AI mentions for unbranded cola queries go to **Coca-Cola and Pepsi**.
> Only 9.0% go to niche brands.

Unless your fractional marketer understands this, they'll run a 2019 playbook in a 2026 market. Traditional brand-strength stops predicting AI visibility — market share and AI share-of-voice are **not correlated**.

Source: Chen et al. (U of Toronto, Sep 2025), McKinsey (Oct 2025)

## The failure mode: Mirage PMF

> **Mirage PMF:** The illusion of product-market fit created by revenue growth that's powered by human labor — not AI leverage.

Every "AI-powered" agency should be asked:

- What's your north-star product metric?
- What's your ARR per employee vs. the legacy incumbent?
- Is your gross margin expanding as you grow?

If they can't answer, they're a services firm with a marketing spin.
