# Government of Telangana

The state government of Telangana, India (capital: Hyderabad). Formed in 2014 after the bifurcation of Andhra Pradesh. Relevant in this wiki as a civic-innovation node.

## Why it's in this wiki

- **[[phani-sama]] served as Chief Innovation Officer** from Nov 2017 to Feb 2020 — mandate: "build a strong foundation for an innovation-driven economy in Telangana." Adjacent to his [[kakatiya-sandbox]] co-founding work.
- **[[siva-cotipalli]]'s home region.** Telugu is Siva's native language; Telangana and Andhra Pradesh are his cultural home even though he's based in Bengaluru + Keller TX today. Siva's Digital Head role for a South-India political organization (2017–2020) also anchored in Hyderabad.
- Both Phani and Siva's Hyderabad-era work (2017–2020) overlap in time — potential network hooks for any ProGrowth India-market public-sector play.
