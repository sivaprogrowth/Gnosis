# Semrush AI Visibility Index (AIVI)

A product/tool from Semrush that benchmarks brand visibility across AI search engines. Publicly accessible interactive dashboard, monthly data refresh. The clearest public reference point for what an AI-search benchmarking tool looks like as a productized offering — highly relevant to ProGrowth's `overviews.progrowth.services` initiative.

## What it measures

- **Share of Voice** per brand per vertical — factoring mention frequency and mention position.
- **Brand Diversity** per vertical — concentration measure (how many brands capture 80% of visibility).
- **Source Diversity** per vertical — unique cited sources per prompt.
- **Co-occurrence** patterns — which brands get named together in AI answers.
- **Cited sources** per vertical — which domains AI pulls from most.

## Engine coverage (as of April 2026 snapshot)

- [[chatgpt]] — primary
- Google AI Mode — a Google AI surface distinct from [[google-ai-overview]] (see note on that page)

**Missing:** [[claude]], [[perplexity]], [[gemini]] (as standalone chatbot). This is a material coverage gap for any brand with audiences on Anthropic or Perplexity.

## Query volume

- 2,500 prompts × 5 industries = 12,500 observation points per monthly refresh.
- Specific industries not publicly enumerated on the overview page.

## Strategic significance for ProGrowth

### As a competitor
Semrush is a well-resourced SaaS incumbent with existing SEO-tool distribution. AIVI is positioned as a free public-facing ranking tool — likely a top-of-funnel lead magnet for Semrush's paid GEO tooling down the road. ProGrowth's `overviews.progrowth.services` needs to be differentiated or it will be commoditized.

### Differentiation levers
- **Broader engine coverage** — include [[claude]], [[perplexity]], [[gemini]]; AIVI is thin here.
- **Deeper verticals** — niche B2B verticals (professional services, credit unions, fractional roles) are underweighted in Semrush's enterprise-consumer focus.
- **Fractional-agency integration** — AIVI is a diagnostic; ProGrowth can bundle the diagnostic with the earned-media execution that moves it. Semrush is unlikely to do service delivery.
- **Challenger-brand framing** — explicitly leverage the [[brand-strength-ai-visibility-gap]] finding to position the tool as the "incumbent-visibility-surplus destroyer".

### As a methodology reference
The SoV + Brand Diversity + Source Diversity metric trio is a good starting skeleton. ProGrowth should adopt equivalent metrics but with:
- Per-engine severity weighting (some engines matter more to the client's ICP).
- Earned-source health scoring (per [[earned-media-bias]]).
- Query-stage classification (awareness / consideration / decision per [[ai-search-decision-journey]]).

## Known limitations

- Proprietary prompt set — customers can't verify query relevance to their own audience.
- Proprietary weighting — SoV formula is described, Brand Diversity/Source Diversity formulas less so.
- Single-shot claim ("4.4× conversion uplift for LLM-search users") — no public primary source.
