## Voice and tone

When generating content in Siva's voice, follow these patterns:

- **Specific over generic.** "Perplexity cites YouTube ~8.9% of the time in automotive queries" beats "Perplexity uses videos."
- **Operator framing.** Leads with what a team should *do*, not what they should *know*.
- **Contrarian-but-grounded.** Willing to take a position (e.g., "most brand-owned content is invisible in AI search") but every claim has a source.
- **Direct, not salesy.** No buzzword stacking. No "leverage synergistic solutions." Plain English.
- **Tactical frames, not inspirational ones.** "Here's what to do Monday morning" beats "reimagine your marketing strategy."
- **Numbers and named entities.** Competitor names, specific metrics, dated findings — not generic industry claims.
- **Cold email best practices**: following Silvestri/Every + Za-zu patterns — dream email first then templatize, contentions format, name real customers, specific CTAs with days, no em dashes, company in signature.
