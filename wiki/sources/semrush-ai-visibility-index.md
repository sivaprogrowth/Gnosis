# Semrush AI Visibility Index (snapshot, April 2026)

## What this source is

The Semrush AI Visibility Index is a **monthly-updated interactive benchmarking tool** — not a research paper — that ranks brand visibility across AI search engines. Discovered via a bit.ly shortlink shared by Elena Verna (growth/PLG writer) in a Semrush newsletter promotion.

This source page is a **snapshot of the April 2026 data release and overview copy**. Because the tool updates monthly, methodology and key claims should be re-verified before citing in client work.

## Methodology (as described on the page)

- **Query volume:** 2,500 prompts across 5 major industries.
- **Engines covered:** [[chatgpt]] and Google AI Mode (distinct from [[google-ai-overview]] — see entity page note).
- **Data points tracked:** brand mentions in LLM answers, mention position, co-occurrence with competitors, cited sources, visibility trends over time.

## Key metrics introduced

- **Share of Voice (SoV)** — combines mention frequency and mention position. Formal definition: *"If a brand was mentioned first in every AI answer, they would achieve an SoV of 100%."* Useful operational metric for [[brand-strength-ai-visibility-gap]] measurement.
- **Brand Diversity** — number of brands that collectively capture 80% of visibility in a vertical. High Brand Diversity = fragmented / competitive market. Low = concentrated / oligopolistic.
- **Source Diversity Score** — unique cited sources ÷ total prompts per vertical. Conceptual cousin to [[citation-network-mapping]]'s set-size measurements.

## Noteworthy claims on the page

- **"LLM-search users convert 4.4× more than those using search engines."** Striking number; **no primary source cited** on the page. Flag as *unverified* when citing in client-facing work. If valid, this is a strong GEO ROI argument.
- AI-generated results projected to overtake organic search by 2028. Aligns with McKinsey's $750B-by-2028 framing in [[new-front-door-to-the-internet]].
- ChatGPT: 2.5B daily prompts from 190M+ users. Different (larger) than figures in [[geo-how-to-dominate-ai-search]] which cited ~3.1B monthly visits / 81% market share — consistent with rapid growth through 2025–2026.

## Strengths and limitations

- ✅ Live/updated data — unlike a static paper, stays current.
- ✅ Interactive — filter by vertical, date, engine.
- ✅ Public-facing brand leaderboards (per-vertical).
- ⚠️ **Narrow engine coverage** — only ChatGPT + Google AI Mode. Misses [[claude]], [[perplexity]], [[gemini]] (as a standalone chatbot).
- ⚠️ Proprietary methodology — prompt set and scoring weights not fully disclosed.
- ⚠️ Unverified conversion claim (4.4×) — provenance unclear from the public overview.

## Relevance to ProGrowth
